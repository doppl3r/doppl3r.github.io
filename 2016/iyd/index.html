What We Do

Financial Scholarships

We will offer financial scholarships, assistance and support where needed on the front end. After the fact we hope to leverage our connections in the Financial Arena to connect families in need today to learn more about cash preservation vehicles so that they one day will be financially sound and sufficient, no longer dependent on Federal monies.

Scholarships will be based on a financial criteria and an application for funding will be required by all those who apply. The Executive Board and/or Director will make the determination for funding.

Opportunities

Ignite Your Dreams-IYD is a forthcoming 501(c)3 public charity in sports, performing arts & music for children ages 5-18. IYD connects the athletics, music and performing arts to the non-profit sector, and has a focus to keeping children in their communities active, away from negative pressure and learning life skills through these types of disciplines.

Our approach in the community is through public service events, programs, exhibitions, training and safety clinics.
